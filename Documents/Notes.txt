Here’s a layout for a custom homepage for an event management app tailored to weddings, birthday parties, and other events, featuring various services like photography, venue, decoration, sound, anchor, pandit, etc.

---

### *App Name: EventEase*

---

### *1. Header / Navigation Bar*

- *Logo*: Display your app’s logo on the top left.
- *Navigation Menu* (right side):
  - Home
  - Services
  - Bookings
  - About Us
  - Contact
  - User Profile (with an avatar or account icon)

- *Call to Action Button*: 
  - "Plan My Event" (highlighted button for quick access to the planning process)
  - Login / Sign Up buttons on the far right.

---

### *2. Hero Section (Main Banner)*

- *Background*: A high-quality, vibrant image or carousel of various events like weddings, birthday parties, corporate events, etc.
- *Main Heading*: "Make Every Event Unforgettable"
- *Subheading*: "Plan your perfect wedding, birthday, or party with top-notch services."
- *Action Buttons*:
  - "Get Started" (for creating new events)
  - "Explore Services" (scrolls down to service section)

---

### *3. Event Type Selection (Quick Links)*

- *Heading*: "What Event Are You Planning?"
- *Icons/Images Grid* (Visual cards with icons or images representing different event types):
  - *Wedding*
  - *Birthday Party*
  - *Corporate Event*
  - *Baby Shower*
  - *Anniversary*
  - *Engagement*
  - *Festival*
  - *Other Special Events*

Each card should have a button "Start Planning" that takes the user to a dedicated page with tailored service options for the selected event.

---

### *4. Featured Services Section*

- *Heading*: "Services We Offer"
- *Grid Layout*: Display service categories with attractive images or icons.
  - *Photography* (Image of a professional photographer in action)
  - *Venue Selection* (Images of beautifully decorated venues)
  - *Decoration* (Vibrant, elegant decoration setup)
  - *Sound & Lighting* (Concert-style lighting and speaker setup)
  - *Anchor* (A person with a mic hosting an event)
  - *Pandit* (Religious figure performing a wedding ritual)
  - *Catering* (Gourmet food platters and servers)
  
Each service should have a "Learn More" button or a quick "Book Now" option.

---

### *5. Popular Event Packages Section*

- *Heading*: "Popular Event Packages"
- *Card-Based Layout*: Show curated event packages for easy booking, each with a featured image, price, and brief description.
  - *Wedding Deluxe Package*
  - *Birthday Party Fun Package*
  - *Corporate Meeting Essentials*
  
Each package card should have a "View Details" button and a "Book Now" option.

---

### *6. Testimonials / Reviews Section*

- *Heading*: "What Our Clients Say"
- *Testimonial Carousel*: Display reviews from happy clients with their names, profile pictures, and event type.
  - For example: "EventNest made my wedding day magical! Everything was perfect from the venue to the catering." - Sarah J., Wedding

---

### *7. How It Works Section*

- *Heading*: "How It Works"
- *Step-by-Step Guide* (with icons for each step):
  1. *Select Event Type*: Choose from weddings, birthdays, and more.
  2. *Choose Services*: Pick the services that suit your event.
  3. *Get a Quote*: Receive an instant quote based on your selections.
  4. *Book Your Event*: Confirm and book your event hassle-free.
  
- *CTA Button*: "Plan My Event Now"

---

### *8. Partners / Sponsors Section*

- *Heading*: "Our Trusted Partners"
- *Logo Carousel*: Display logos of venues, caterers, photographers, etc., that partner with the app.

---

### *9. Footer Section*

- *Quick Links*:
  - About Us
  - Services
  - Careers
  - Terms & Conditions
  - Privacy Policy
  
- *Contact Info*:
  - Email
  - Phone Number
  - Social Media Icons (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc.)
  
- *Newsletter Subscription*: "Stay updated with the latest events and offers" with a field to input email and subscribe.

---

### *Color Scheme & Visual Style*
- *Primary Color*: Elegant, celebratory tones like gold, deep purple, or royal blue.
- *Accent Colors*: Use bright accents like white, blush pink, or turquoise for emphasis on buttons and icons.
- *Typography*: Clean, modern fonts like Montserrat or Roboto for a professional yet festive look.

This homepage design offers easy navigation, attractive visuals, and a user-friendly experience for planning events efficiently and booking services.
